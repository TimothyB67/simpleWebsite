About The Industry

  1. The company that I am working in is called BetterHelp. BetterHelp is part of the online/virtual mental-health services industry.

  2. It offers therapy sessions via text, chat, phone or video through a web/app platform.

  3. It also connects licensed therapists with clients remotely.

Career in this Organization

  2. As the Head of Marketing I will come up with marketing strategies across major channels such as paid media to suppor engagement and brand value.

  3. I will also manage budgets and marketing operations with optimization.

Ethical Issues

  1. An ethical issue that could arise from my position as the Head of Marketing could be a privacy and data ethic issue.

  2. The BetterHelp organization collects sensitive information about the users.

  3. If that sensitive and personal information were to be advertised for marketing purposes, it could break the trust that users have with the organization.

Cybersecurity Issues

  1. One cybersecurity issue that could arise is a data breach. The Better Help organization has a large amount of personally identifiable information (PII) and protected health information (PHI). If a cyber attack were to occur that sensitive data and infromation would be at risk.

  2. Another cyber security issue that could arise is a third-party risk. BetterHelp relies on third-party partners for analytics, payment processing, video chat, advertising, and customer support. If any of these partners experience a security incident or misuse shared data, it could compromise BetterHelp users.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

  1. The BetterHelp transmits and store large amounts of Protected Health Information (PHI).

  2. It would be good practice for the organization to encrypt data at rest and in transit, end-to-endencryption for therapies, and minimize the data that is stored by collecting only what is necessary.

  3. BetterHelp being a teletherapy platfrom, it uses vendors for analytics, payments, customer service, and video hosting. But if a third party vendro were to have a security incident, it could compromise users data.

  4. It would be best practice to continuosly monitor vendors and re-evalutate them for performance and compliance.
