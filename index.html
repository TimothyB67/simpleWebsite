My Top Three Personal Values

Respect

  1. All throughout my life I have known that you should respect everyone regardless of how you feel about them.

  2. Respect is a two way street, in order to receive it, you must give it.

  3. Having respect is important because you should treat others the way you want to be treated

  4. If you don't show others respect they will not respect you either.

Accountability

  1. I think that you should always take accountibility for your actions.

  2. If you aren't doing what your supposed to be doing in a work place and you get in trouble, take accountibility for your actions.

  3. Everyone should be accountable for their own actions whether if they're right or wrong.

  4. when you dont take accountibility for doing or not doing something you would be lettin people down.

Honesty

  1. I believe that people should tell the truth even when they are wrong.

  2. Growing up I realized that if you are not honest about something, it can get you into even more trouble.

  3. People should admit what they do even when they are wrong.

  4. If you are not honest about your actions, it can get you into even more trouble or stop you from learning.
